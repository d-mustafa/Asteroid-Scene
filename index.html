Asteroid Scene

Q-Key: Speed Up Asteroids

E-Key: Slow Down Asteroids

Q-Key + E-Key: Stop Asteroids

Left-Arrow-Key: Rotate Asteroids Counter Clockwise

Left-Arrow-Key + Q-Key: Speed Up Asteroids Counter Clockwise

Left-Arrow-Key + E-Key: Slow Down Asteroids Counter Clockwise

W-Key: Speed Up Time

S-Key: Slow Down Time
